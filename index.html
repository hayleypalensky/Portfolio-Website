About Me

Hello, I’m Hayley Palensky, a marketing and graphic design professional with a master’s degree in experiential design. My work is driven by a passion for creating intuitive impactful designs. With a focus on clarity and user experience, I strive to bridge the gap between creativity and functionality. My portfolio showcases a range of projects that blend technical expertise with innovative design thinking. Outside of my professional life, I’m an advocate for personal growth—after graduation, I challenged myself to confront my anxiety head-on by traveling solo across Europe for two months, all while working remotely. I’m energetic, dedicated, and deeply committed to making meaningful connections through my work.
